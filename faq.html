Frequently Asked Questions

Can medication completely relieve tooth pain?

No. Professional dental treatment is needed to address the root cause.

Does scaling damage teeth?

No. Scaling removes plaque and tartar safely without damaging enamel.

Can painful teeth be treated with fillings?

Not always. Deep infections may require root canal treatment.

How can I maintain good oral hygiene?

Brush twice daily, floss regularly, and visit your dentist every six months.

Do all wisdom teeth need removal?

No. Only impacted or problematic wisdom teeth require extraction.

How often should I visit a dentist?

A routine dental checkup every six months is recommended.
